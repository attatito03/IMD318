Education

"Education isn't just about filling a mind with facts; it's about igniting a passion for lifelong learning and empowering individuals to think critically and creatively."



Kindergarden

My education started at Kiddy's Playskool Kindergarden. I went

to that kindergarten for two years and learned a lot of things,

mostly English, because the kindergarten encouraged kids to

speak English, which is why my English has been pretty decent.



Primary School

I continue my journey at Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Layar,

where I learn a lot of things and start my bowling career at the

age of 8 years old. This school appreciates my effort and

contribution to the school. This school is also where I found a

lot of my neighborhood friends because the school is so close

to my house.



High School

In my teenage days, I entered this prestigious school, Sekolah

Menengah Kebangsaan Khir Johari. The school is famous

because of sports. The school is so excellent in sports that

most of the athletes in Sungai Petani choose to enter it, and

people even refer to it as a sports school. In this school, I

become more mature and learn more about life and how to

survive in this world.



University

I got accepted to Univeristi Teknologi Mara, also known as

UiTM. At this university, I learn more about responsibilities and

try to act more like an adult. The university vibe is really

different from the previous education place, but I still adapt

and find a way to make this university era fun by finding new

friends with the same interests or finding new things to do.

This time, I also learned how to play proper basketball.
